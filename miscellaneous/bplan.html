Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • Each team should consist of a minimum of 2 members and a maximum of 4 members. Any number of business plans from an institution may be allowed, provided that the teams have no common members.
  • There will be a preliminary round from which only 7 teams will be selected and be able to present their Business Plan on the day of the event.

Round 1 Rules: Preliminary Round

  • Team leader has to submit a summary of their business through provided mail.
    • What is your product?
    • Its use or the problem that the product tries to address
  • All teams should send their summary by 5th February 2023. On this mail: bplanaquaregia@gmail.com
  • Only 7 teams will be selected. Selected teams will be notified by 26th January 2022.
  • Document header should contain
    • College name:
    Product/ Business name:
    Name and contact number of the team leader:
    Email of the team leader:
    Summary

  • PDF / Word file name should be named in this format while mailing CollegeName_Business/ProductName

Final Round

  • Each team will get 7 minutes to present their idea.
  • You have to present the idea through PPT.
  • After presentation Judges will raise their queries which will also be marked while awarding marks.
  • Use of un-parliamentary language will lead to disqualification.
  • The decision of Judges will be binding.
  • Final decision making authority regarding the competition will rest in the hands of team AQUAREGIA.

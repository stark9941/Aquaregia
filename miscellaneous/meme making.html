Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • Meme templates will be provided to participants via Whatsapp group.
  • The meme must not contain words or subjects that might be offensive to any culture/language/religion/region.
  • Participants have to mail their meme before 11:00 A.M. on 28th March.
  • The meme of the participants will be uploaded on Instagram handle of Aquaregia till 1:00 P.M. on 28th March.
  • Meme should only be in form of jpg or jpeg. Short videos/reels are not allowed.
  • Number of likes on meme will get counted till 11:59 A.M. of 29th March.
  • If any rule mentioned above is violated or the meme is submitted after the given deadline then the entry will be declared invalid.
  • Rules may get modulated under certain circumstances.
    • Judgement criteria:
    • 40% marks will be on the basis of number of likes on post.
    • 60% marks on the basis of creativity, uniqueness, entertainment.
  • The decision of the Judges will be final and binding.

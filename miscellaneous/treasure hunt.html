Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • Maximum of 2 Teams Are Allowed Per Institute.
  • There should be 3-5 members per team.
  • There will be 2 Rounds
  • Round 1 is an elimination Round.
  • Only 5 teams will qualify for the second Round of the Treasure Hunt.
  • Use of the mobile will be prohibited during Activities.
  • No Damage should be done to any of the college premises.
  • Rules for each Round will be told on spot.
  • Judges decision will be final and binding.
  • Rules may get modulated under certain circumstances by the authorities.
    • The event will be conducted in two rounds.
    • Round 1 - Elimination Round(Online)
    • Round 2 - Showcase Round

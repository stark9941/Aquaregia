University Maharaja's College

is an institution in Jaipur, the heart of Rajasthan. Established in 1884 by

Sawai Ram Singh III

, it has been affiliated with the University of Rajasthan since 1947. The college was granted the status of "College with Potential for Excellence" by UGC in 2011.

It is one of the

oldest colleges in North India

and was designed by British architects . The Royal look of building boasts its

heritage history

.

From 40 students in 1875 to 3000+ students currently, Maharaja's family has continued to grow stronger.

The college ground has witnessed several national and international sports events, including the cricket match played by MCC in 1961 where Mr. Salim Durani's performance stole the spotlight.

The college celebrated its

178 years of prosperity

in 2022.

Being a Government institution Maharaja's is a gathering of meritorious students. Despite the common preudice, we are not just an assembly of book-warms, rather we are just as active in cocurricular activities and

AQUAREGIA

is a testament to this.

theme

that

describes our journey and goal, 'Infinty'

.
AQUAREGIA -2023

Aquaregia is an annual inter-college cultural fest of one of Rajasthan's biggest science institutes,

"University Maharaja College"

, which has its roots back in 2016. It all began in 2013 when a group of enthusiastic students from our college decided that there should be more to college life than just textbooks. They formed a Cultural team that participated in various national-level events.

The team took another step by organizing various events, providing a platform for every college student to realize their true potential. A year passed, and the team had grown from 3 to 100+ members, participating in 10 distinct level events in Dance, Drama, Quiz, Debate, and several others. Our predecessors were now aiming even higher and wanting to call something their own, so they started

Aquaregia.

To us it's not just an annual event, It's an emotion.

The name itself

carries with it the core philosophy that alone we might not be able to achieve much, but with the right union of people, we can dissolve our problems. With

25+ colleges and 300+ participants, Aquaregia 2016

was a success.

Aquaregia 2021

was held virtually following the Covid guidelines, but every cloud has a silver lining, as we got

global participants from 6 countries along with national entries from 17 states

.

Aquaregia 2022,

was a great success, thanks to the hard work of the team, we received an awesome response with a footfall of

4 to 5 thousand students from more than 50 colleges

, not only from Jaipur or Rajasthan but also from prestigious colleges of Delhi University.

The 7th edition of the fest served Two power-packed days with

30+ events

among which few were never seen in any of the colleges in Jaipur.

With celebrities like

Miss. Jodhpur Isha Agarwal

and

social media sensation Smiriti Roy,

Aquaregia 2022, was a grand success, with participants not only taking trophies but hearts full of memories.

Years have passed and now, about

150 members

strong Cultural Team have already bagged

175 plus

trophies and titles in their cabinet.

Now it's time for

AQUAREGIA 2023,

the 8th edition, our tagline 'To The Infinity' truly shows the limits of our ambition. Following the legacy of our seniors, we now wish to give further heights to this stunning amalgamation of hard work and emotions. And in this journey we want you to be with us every single step.

Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • There can be multiple registrations from a college/university
  • The time limit for the performance will be 3 to 4 minutes.
  • Negative marking will be exercised for exceeding the time limit.
  • All dance forms are allowed, including classical, semi- classical, folk, and any western style â€“ hip hop, Bollywood, etc.
  • Usage of props is allowed.
  • Vulgarity and obscenity at any point are strictly prohibited and can lead to disqualification. Performance would be judged based on costumes, choreography, expressions, coordination, energy, and creativity.
  • Rules may get modulated under certain circumstances by the authorities.
  • The judges' decision will be final and binding.

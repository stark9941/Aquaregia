Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • Only 2 teams are allowed per college with letterhead. There can be a minimum of 5 and a maximum of 16 members in a team.
  • The time limit for the performance will be 4 to 7 min.
  • Negative marking will be exercised for exceeding the time limit and team members.
  • All dance forms are allowed, including classical, semi- classical and folk and any western style â€“ hip hop, Bollywood etc.
  • Usage of props are allowed.
  • Performance would be judged based on costumes, choreography, expressions, coordination, energy, and creativity.
  • Vulgarity and obscenity at any point is strictly prohibited and can lead to disqualification, song to be chosen accordingly.
  • The judges' decision will be final and binding.
  • Rules may get modulated under certain circumstances by the authorities.

Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • Last date to register is 5 February 2023 till 12 noon.
  • On spot registration are allowed.
  • Maximum two entries are allowed per college.
  • Maximum 5 participants are allowed per team.
  • Exceeding the time limit will lead to disqualification.
  • Reporting time 9:00 am.
  • Participant will have to complete their project in 4.5 hour.
  • Topic will be given on the spot.
  • You can either make an app or an website to complete the given task.
  • You have to design 5 page website on the given topic and in given time.
  • You must bring your own laptops and other required devices along with you for the event.
  • Software to be used : (NOTEPAD, VISUAL STUDIO, PHOTOSHOP)
  • Programming Languages: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
  • Judges decision will be final and binding.
  • Rules may get modulated under certain circumstances by the authorities.

Rules & Regulations

First round:

  • Participants will have to recite a self-composed poem without using any paper or other source.
  • candidates can use both English and Hindi languages for the event.
  • The time limit to recite poetry is maximum 3 minutes.
  • Maximum of two entries are allowed per college.

SECOND round :

  • Top 8 participants will move forward for this round.
  • Rules and Regulations of Second Round will be announced on the spot.
  • Marks from 1st Round will be carried forward to the second round.
  • Plagiarism will not be tolerated.
  • Indecent language , hurting the sentiment of a particular community will lead to disqualification.
  • Final decision making power regarding the competition will be in the hands of team AQUAREGIA.
  • Judges decision will be binding .

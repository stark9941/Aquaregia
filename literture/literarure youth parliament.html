Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • Registered Participants will be allotted portfolios of Members of Lok Sabha by the team Aquaregia.
  • Background guide for the committee will be mailed to the participants 2 days prior to the event.
  • This event is a singleton event and not a team event.
  • There are a limited number of portfolios only , so it will be distributed on a first come first serve basis.
  • Your participation will be considered final only when you have received a mail from us after registration.
  • No request to change in the portfolio will be considered under any circumstances.
  • Process mentioned in the background guide may vary with the actual process in the parliament.
  • Participants are required to thoroughly cross check the time table in order to avoid any clashes with the other events.
  • On the spot entry is not permitted and applicable for this event.
  • The candidates can use both English and Hindi languages while speaking but the documentation will only be done in English.
  • In case of any doubt please contact the event coordinator.
  • In case of any dispute, the decision of judges and team AQUAREGIA will prevail.

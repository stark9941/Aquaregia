Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • The event will be conducted in 2 Rounds.
  • The first round will be an elimination round on the first day of the event and participants have to write an essay on the given topic with a word limit of 500.
  • The top 40% of participants will proceed to the second round which will conduct on the second day of the event.
  • In the second round, the participant will be given a hypothetical situation to write upon.
  • Participants can use either English or Hindi language.
  • The entry must not contain any obscene words or subject(s) that might be offensive to any culture/ language/ region/ religion.
  • Judgement criteria: Creativity, Writing and thinking capability
  • Rules may get modulated under certain circumstances by the authorities.
  • The decision of the organizers and judges will be final and cannot be changed.

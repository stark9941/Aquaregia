Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • Only 1 group entry will be allowed from an institution.
  • The limit of team members would be 5 to 8 members.
  • The team can include Classical, Bollywood, Qawwali, or a fusion of songs.
  • The preferred languages for the songs are: - Hindi, Rajasthani, Punjabi, and English.
  • The time limit for each performance will be 15 min including setup.
  • Exceeding the time limit will lead to negative marking.
  • The group will have to bring their own instruments.
  • Karaoke Music is not allowed.
  • Foul language should not be used.
  • Judging Criteria: Coordination, Creativity, Quality of voice, Sense of scale and beat, Quality of rendition Improvisation, Pronunciation of lyrics, Mood and expression, Overall impact.
  • The judges' decision will be final.
  • Rules may get modulated under certain circumstances by the authorities.

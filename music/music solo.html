Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • Number of participants can be 1 or 2 (1 singer and 1 accompanist if required).
  • Participants can sing any song of their choice provided decency is maintained at all time.
  • The Preferred languages for songs are: - Hindi, Rajasthani, Punjabi and English.
  • The time limit for each performance will be 4 minutes.
  • Exceeding the time limit will lead to disqualification.
  • Participants should bring their own instruments if required.
  • Karaoke music is allowed.
  • Foul language should not be used.
  • Judging Criteria: Quality of voice, Sense of scale and beat, Quality of rendition Improvisation, Pronunciation of lyrics, Mood and expression, Overall impact.
  • Judges decision will be final and binding.
  • Rules may get modulated under certain circumstances by the authorities.
    • The event will be conducted in two rounds.
    • Round 1 - Elimination Round(Online)
    • Round 2 - Showcase Round

Round 1 Rules: Elimination Round(Online)

  • This is an elimination round.
  • The participants have to send a recorded video. The duration of the video should not exceed the time limit of 3 Minutes.
  • Participants must rename their video as - Name_CollegeName_ContactNo.
  • The Participants will be judged on the basis of their recorded videos.
  • The recorded video can be submitted on the Email ID given below:
    aquaregiasinging@gmail.com
    OR
    to the respective Coordinators.

  • Submission Deadline: 4 February 2023.
  • Voice clarity should be there in the video and it should be recorded in one go.
  • Any kind of voiceover will not be allowed.
  • Background music will be allowed and participants can also use their own instruments while singing.

Round 2 Rules: Showcase Round

  • Selected participants from round 1 will proceed to the second round which will be conducted in college campus.
  • Participants can either perform the same singing performance of round 1 or as per their choice.
  • The time range for each performance would be 3-4 minutes.

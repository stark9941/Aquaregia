Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • Time limit for each performance is 15-18 minutes.
  • There will be Negative marking for exceeding the time limit.
  • Team size: Maximum 15 members (with instruments player).
  • Only one entry is allowed from an institute.
  • Teams can use banners and posters.
  • Teams are allowed to use instruments of their choices.
  • Mic usage will not be allowed in any circumstances.
  • Teams can choose the topic /theme by their own.
  • Use of abusive language will lead the team to disqualification.
  • Rules may get modulated under certain circumstances by the authorities.
  • Judgement criteria:
    • Coordination
    • Crowd interaction
    • Facial expression
    • Social message
    • Overall performance
  • The judges' decision will be final and binding.

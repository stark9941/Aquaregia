Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • Only 2 teams per college will be allowed.
  • The team members limit should be 8 to 12 members.
  • The time limit would be 7 to 11 minutes for each performance.
  • There is no particular theme.
  • Vulgarity and obscenity at any point are strictly prohibited and can lead to disqualification.
  • Judgment Criteria:
    • Costume
    • Creativity
    • Theme
    • Stage appearance
  • Rules may get modulated under certain circumstances by the authorities.
  • The decision of judges will be final and binding.
